Exam Resources

The DECA written test is 100 question multiple choice exam. You have 70 minutes to complete the test. The Regionals qualifier exam is written on Oct. 31st at 3:15 - dont be late. There will also be an exam at provincials. Make sure that you practice the regionals practice exams for the qualifier and the provincials practices for provincials.
Remember that principles = business administration, management = business management and admin

Case Study Resources

In the DECA case study, invididual participants are given 10 minutes to prepare for a case study and 10 minutes to present, with up to 3 questions being asked at the end by the judge. Teams get 30 minutes to preapre for a 15 minute presentation. You will receive your case at the start of the prep period, then you will hand it back at the end and proceed to the judging area. The best way to study for the case study is to practice previous case studies and to research the performance indicators, as they are provided.

Performance indicators:

  • Finance PIs
  • Entrepenurship PIs
  • Marketing PIs
  • Hospitality PIs
  • Business Management PIs
